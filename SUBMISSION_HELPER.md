Here's a summary of the provided information for a WordPress.org submission:

1.  **Plugin Name:** Royal Login Architect

2.  **Short Description:** A unique PHP-only WordPress utility for customizing the login page with royal style, focusing on simplicity and efficiency.

3.  **Detailed Description:**
    Royal Login Architect empowers WordPress administrators to transform their default login page into a custom-branded experience. This PHP-only plugin offers a straightforward yet powerful suite of options to customize various elements of the login screen without touching a single line of code.

    **Key Features:**
    *   **Customizable Login Logo:** Choose between an elegant image logo (supporting base64 encoded SVGs, with a default crown icon provided) or a prominent text logo with adjustable color.
    *   **Extensive Color Options:** Easily change the background color of the entire login page, the login form itself, and even the login button's background and text colors.
    *   **Custom Login Message:** Display a personalized message above the login form, supporting basic HTML for flexible communication with users.
    *   **Branded Header Link:** The login logo will link to your site's home URL, and its tooltip will display your custom logo text, ensuring consistent branding.
    *   **Seamless Integration:** All settings are managed through a dedicated, user-friendly "Royal Login Architect" page under the WordPress Settings menu.
    *   **Lightweight & Efficient:** Built purely with PHP, the plugin avoids unnecessary external scripts, ensuring optimal performance and compatibility.

    Royal Login Architect acts as your personal designer, giving you full control over the aesthetic and messaging of your WordPress login gateway. It's ideal for agencies, businesses, or anyone looking to provide a more polished and professional entry point to their WordPress site.

4.  **GitHub URL:** https://github.com/ogichanchan/royal-login-architect
